Established in 2017, Parna Kuteera is a sanctuary where the rhythm of the waves meets the comfort of home. Located directly opposite the pristine Nirvana Beach, we offer a tranquil "hideaway" designed for those looking to disconnect and rejuvenate.

Prime Location

Seconds away from the secluded Nirvana Beach sands.

Personalized Care

Bespoke itineraries crafted by our experienced local team.

Cultural Soul

Enriching experiences that blend heritage with coastal vibes.

Whether you seek a quiet solo retreat or a vibrant family holiday, Parna Kuteera promises an unforgettable experience that will leave you refreshed and inspired.

Accommodation

Choose Your Perfect Stay

Family Room

4 Occupancy

Spacious and comfortable family rooms designed for your loved ones. Enjoy quality time together with modern amenities and a cozy atmosphere perfect for creating cherished family memories.

Dormitory

10 Occupancy

Perfect for groups and budget travelers. Our dormitory offers a friendly communal experience with clean, well-maintained facilities, ideal for adventurers and backpackers exploring the coast.

Tent Stay

Beach Front

Experience ultimate adventure with our tent stays right in front of the beach. Fall asleep to the sound of waves and wake up to breathtaking ocean views. A unique glamping experience awaits!
