Overview

Welcome to NIRVANA Trekk and Travel – where adventure meets serenity on the magnificent coasts of Karnataka!

Experience the Unknown

Escape the hustle and bustle of Bangalore and immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of Gokarna's pristine beaches. Our meticulously planned 2-day coastal adventure takes you on an unforgettable journey through some of India's most stunning seaside destinations.

From thrilling water sports activities like kayaking and deep-sea swimming to exploring hidden caves and spotting dolphins in their natural habitat, every moment of your journey will be filled with wonder and excitement. Meet fellow adventure enthusiasts, share stories around a crackling campfire under the stars, and experience the unique dancing-in-rain joy with our signature rain dance activities.

Your Journey Awaits

Start your weekend Friday night from Bangalore with early morning check-in at the beachfront Parna Kuteera. Enjoy quality time with boating adventures, temple visits, and authentic beach experiences. Spend your nights under the stars in beachfront camping, indulge in delicious meals, and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Whether you're a solo traveler seeking new connections, a couple looking for romance, or friends seeking adventure – NIRVANA Trekk and Travel offers the perfect beach escape with activities for everyone. Join us as we explore Gokarna Temple, navigate the historic Mirjan Port, experience the spiritual Murudeshwar, and witness the serene mangrove walks of Honnavar.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Gokarna?

The best time to visit Gokarna is between October and May when the weather is pleasant and suitable for beach activities. Avoid monsoon season (June-September) due to rough seas and heavy rainfall.

Is this trip suitable for beginners?

Yes! All activities included in this trip are beginner-friendly. Our experienced trip leaders will guide you through every activity, whether it's kayaking, swimming, or cave exploration. No prior experience is necessary.

What should I pack for this trip?

Pack light, breathable clothing, swimwear, a towel, sunscreen, sunglasses, water shoes, a light jacket for evening, personal toiletries, medications, and a camera. Avoid excessive baggage as space is limited in vehicles.

Can I bring my own food or have dietary restrictions?

We can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, and other dietary preferences with advance notice. Please inform us at the time of booking so we can arrange suitable meal options for you.

How far is Gokarna from Bangalore?

Gokarna is approximately 480 kilometers from Bangalore. The journey takes about 9-10 hours by road. We provide comfortable overnight travel with AC vehicles to make your journey pleasant.

Is the trip suitable for solo travelers?

Absolutely! Our trips are specifically designed for solo travelers to meet new people, make friends, and share experiences. We have created a safe and welcoming community environment for all.

What safety measures are in place during water activities?

All water activities are conducted with certified instructors and safety equipment. Life jackets are provided for all participants. Our team ensures adherence to all safety protocols and weather conditions.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, cancellations are accepted up to 7 days before the trip for a full refund. Cancellations within 7 days may have partial refund depending on our cancellation policy. Please contact us for complete details.

Is travel insurance included?

Travel insurance is not included in the package. We recommend purchasing travel insurance separately for protection against unforeseen circumstances and medical emergencies.

What's included in the breakfast?

Breakfasts include a variety of options such as toast, eggs, tea, coffee, fruits, and local specialties. Special dietary requirements like vegan or gluten-free can be arranged with prior notice.
