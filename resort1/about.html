About TownLink Resort

Where Luxury Meets Coastal Paradise

Our Story

Since opening our doors in 2010, TownLink Resort has been a beacon of luxury and hospitality on the pristine shores of Paradise Bay. What began as a vision to create an unparalleled beachfront sanctuary has evolved into one of the region's most celebrated luxury destinations.

Our commitment to excellence, sustainability, and genuine hospitality has earned us numerous accolades, including the prestigious Five-Star Diamond Award and recognition as one of the world's top beach resorts by leading travel publications.
