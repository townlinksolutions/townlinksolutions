Medical emergencies can be financially devastating. Our health insurance plans ensure you and your family have access to quality medical care without worrying about costs.

Key Benefits:

  • ✓ Cashless hospitalization at network hospitals

  • ✓ Pre-existing condition coverage
  • ✓ Maternity and new-born coverage

Motor Insurance

Protect your vehicle against damages, theft, and third-party liabilities with comprehensive motor insurance coverage. Whether you own a car, bike, or commercial vehicle, we have the right plan for you.

Key Benefits:

  • ✓ Third-party liability coverage (mandatory)
  • ✓ Own damage coverage for your vehicle
  • ✓ Protection against theft and accidents
  • ✓ Cashless claim settlement at authorized garages
  • ✓ Add-on covers (Personal accident, roadside assistance)
  • ✓ Fast claim processing

Who Should Buy:

Motor insurance is mandatory for all vehicle owners in India. Whether you drive daily or occasionally, comprehensive coverage protects you legally and financially.

Term Insurance

Get maximum life protection at minimum premiums with affordable term insurance plans. Pure protection with no investment component means more coverage for your family at lower costs.

Key Benefits:

  • ✓ High coverage at affordable premiums (₹50 lakhs to ₹1 crore)
  • ✓ Simple and transparent policy terms
  • ✓ Multiple term options (10, 20, 30 years)
  • ✓ Increasing and decreasing term options
  • ✓ Accidental death benefit riders
  • ✓ Disability and critical illness riders

Who Should Buy:

Young professionals, working parents, and anyone with financial dependents should prioritize term insurance to ensure their family's financial security.
